Frequently Asked Questions

What is educational counselling?

Educational counselling is a process where trained professionals provide guidance to students about their academic choices, career paths, and personal development. It helps students make informed decisions about their education and future career.

How long does a counselling session last?

A typical counselling session lasts for 45-60 minutes. However, the duration may vary depending on the complexity of the issues being discussed and the type of counselling service you've opted for.

How can I reschedule or cancel my booking?

To reschedule or cancel your booking, please contact us at least 24 hours before your scheduled session. You can email us at counselling@collegegate.com or call us at +91 135 2741487. Rescheduling is subject to counsellor availability.

What should I prepare before a counselling session?

Before your session, it's helpful to have your academic records, list of interests, career aspirations, and any specific questions you'd like to discuss. This will make the session more productive and tailored to your needs.

Do you offer group counselling sessions?

Yes, we offer group counselling sessions for schools, colleges, and other educational institutions. These sessions are customized based on the needs of the group and cover topics like career exploration, exam preparation, stress management, etc. Please contact us for more details.
